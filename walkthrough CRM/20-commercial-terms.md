# §20. Commercial terms

**Spec (Build Specification §20):** The SOW operates under the Enterprise Agreement dated 15 May 2026. All commercial terms — implementation fee, monthly minimum commitment, usage thresholds, initial term, the 3-month Proof of Concept and the Month 3 break clause — are set out in Appendix A of that Agreement and are deliberately not restated in the SOW; Appendix A governs. Additional workflows, integrations or functionality beyond the described scope may be scoped and agreed separately, per Appendix A.

## Where this stands

- This section is contractual, not technical — there is nothing in the demo build that implements or contradicts it. No commercial figures are reproduced anywhere in this repo; the SOW extraction (`docs/SOW.md` §20) preserves the pointer to Appendix A without restating numbers.
- The "scoped separately" clause is the commercial home for anything the client asks for beyond the spec. Candidates already visible from the build and discovery trackers: Advisory Engagement if confirmed in-scope (Q14), any integrations beyond the §14 list, and production-hardening items in `memory/remaining-tasks.md` if they exceed the PoC's agreed shape.
- The 3-month PoC and Month 3 break clause referenced here frame the §16 timeline: the 4–6-week implementation sits inside that PoC window, and the deferred-work ledger is effectively the list of what must land before live PoC operations.
