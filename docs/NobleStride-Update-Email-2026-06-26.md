## What we're building now

*Each item maps directly to the feedback you gave us.*

- **Investor engagement pipeline** — a stage-based view (Shared → Teaser → NDA → IM → VDR → Due Diligence → Term Sheet → Offer → Invested / Declined), with filters and drill-down into each source deal.
- **Partner fee-share lifecycle** — referral tracking from introduction through to disbursement, including fee-sharing agreement and terms, NDA / partner-agreement status, and conversion reporting.
- **Investor & Partner portals** — controlled external views. Investors see only matching opportunities, gated by engagement stage. Other investors' identities, partner identities, internal notes, and engagement contracts are never exposed.
- **Role-based access** — a viewpoint switcher (Admin / Investor / Partner) to see the CRM through each lens, plus an in-organisation access matrix (Admin / Deal Lead / Team Member) showing who sees what internally.
- **Document handling** — NDAs, engagement contracts, teasers, IMs, term sheets and more, tracked with access levels (Internal / Client-shared / Investor-shared / VDR) that drive the portal gating.
- **Branding & project codenames** — "Noblestride Capital" applied throughout, with project codenames surfaced in the UI.
